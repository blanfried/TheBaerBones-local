MY APPROACH

I am a Solutions Architect with degrees in Psychology and Philosophy.

I solve problems by understanding you first — your goals, your constraints, and your desired outcome.

Then I build the solution that works.

Keep scrolling to see my process.
this is me
Step 1

Alignment.

Every project is different—ranging from an initial website build to a comprehensive digital home base where your business operates, markets, and sells.

But every project starts the same: we begin with a deep conversation about your goals, constraints, and desired outcome.

Then I'll map out everything that needs to happen and bring it back to you so we can move forward together.

From there, we both know exactly what needs to be built.

Step 2

Architecture.

Once we're aligned on what needs to happen, I design how it will happen.

I'll research and select the right tools and technologies.

Then I'll map out how you, your team, and your customers will interact with every part of the system, ensuring it is simple and intuitive.

Finally, I'll create the technical blueprint that becomes our roadmap for Step 3: Implementation.

Learn More
Step 3

Implementation.

With our roadmap in hand, I build your solution.

I'll start with the core and work outward, testing each component as I go, making sure everything functions as intended.

Throughout the process, I'll keep you updated and make adjustments based on performance and your input.

Once complete, I'll deploy your solution and walk you through how it all works. From there, it scales with your business, and when you need to expand or start something new, I'll be happy to help.
