My Philosophy

Shallow design starts with the product.

The best design begins with story.

My background is in Ethics and Psychology. I want to understand you — your history, your ethos, and the "why" behind your business.

Step 1

Connection.

Let’s have a conversation. We’ll get to know each other and I’ll learn more about your goals, your values, and what you need from a website.

My tagline is Build Your Purpose for a reason — I design best when I understand the heart of your work.
‍
After our chat, I’ll send over a quote, and if we’re aligned, we’ll move on to Step 2: Creation.

Meet With Me
Step 2

Creation.

First, I'll collect more info from you.

Then, I'll put everything I've gathered into a first draft. You'll go through it and let me know what you think, be thorough!

We'll repeat the process with a second draft. Then, depending on the type of website, I'll either hand it over to you or host it for you.

Et voila, your brand new website is good to go! 🎉

Learn More
Step 3

Collaboration.

Once finished, I'll make the website handoff as smooth as possible.

Whilst I don't do website management, I won't leave you out to dry if you need me.

If you need help on other technical matters, I'll gladly pass on your inquiry to a specialist I work with.

Eventually, I'd like to start a community where we can all collaborate, trade services, and generally help each other!
