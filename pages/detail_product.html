02
What Kind of Websites Do You Build?
X

At THEBAERBØNES, we build two types of websites: custom-coded websites and CMS-based websites.

• Custom-Coded Websites: These websites are built from scratch, giving you maximum flexibility in design and features. They are perfect for clients who need a static website to act as a professional home base—a place to showcase their services or portfolio, and provide essential contact information. Custom-coded sites are ideal if you don’t plan to update content regularly or post new material frequently. With a static site, you can focus on your business while I take care of the design and structure.
‍
• CMS-Based Websites: CMS (Content Management System) websites are built on platforms like Shopify, Squarespace, Wix, WordPress.com, Webflow, BigCommerce, and others. These websites are a great choice for business owners who want to manage their website independently—whether that means running an online store or posting blog content regularly. CMS sites come with built-in tools that make it easy to update your site without technical expertise.
‍
If your needs involve frequent updates, product uploads, or regular posting, a CMS website may be the best fit, as it gives you more control. However, if your needs are simpler and you’re looking for a set-and-forget website to showcase your business, a custom-coded site might be the better option.
‍
If you’re unsure which option to go with, we would be happy to discuss your goals to help you make the right choice. Feel free to reach out to us anytime at contact@TheBaerBones.com or scroll down a bit to send us a message!
