About Me

‍

I (Baer) am a Solutions Architect and Technical Product Manager bridging technical complexity with enterprise business impact across multiple industries.

I serve as Solutions Architect for a global manufacturer's ($30-40M revenue) enterprise e-commerce platform facilitating $2M monthly sales ($24M annually), I align 30+ stakeholders across 5 departments to deliver platform updates and system integrations. I coach team members on architectural patterns and solution design through systems thinking methodology. I also advise enterprise B2B partnerships between the organization and partner companies of 1,000-7,000+ employees (including Rooms To Go), traveling with C-suite to deliver technical presentations and solution designs.

Recently delivered a $350K-scoped platform modernization delivered in 6 months well under budget. Platform improvements influencing ~$100K/month in additional revenue, achieving 81%→52% homepage bounce reduction and generating $90K+ annual operational savings. Featured in Furniture World, Home News Now, Furniture Today.

As Technical Product Manager for a SaaS startup, Vinissimo Enology, I co-architected AWS multi-tenant SaaS infrastructure for projected 50+ enterprise clients.
