AICO Website Rebuild

AICO, a $100 million/year furniture manufacturer, had a burgeoning $200k problem. Their website was developed over the course of 25+ years by IT professionals, sans documentation. It was an inextricable twine of internal connections. No one at AICO touched it beyond minor photo updates for fear of breaking or causing a security risk.

The CEO and founder, Michael Amini, demanded an update. Referring to AICO's opulent showrooms in High Point and Las Vegas, Michael stated, "Our website should awe people the same way our showrooms do".

For these reasons, amongst others, external vendors quoted $200k for the desired rebuild. I delivered the final product in adherence to every constraint for under $1000.
