Core Values

The principles I hold myself to personally and professionally:

  • I believe that truth, in all its forms, creates better lives for everyone.
  • I seek clarity and understanding before proposing judgments or solutions.
  • I examine every problem from multiple perspectives until I understand its context.
  • I use the principles and best practices from philosophy, psychology, ethics, programming, and automation to create functional solutions to any problem.
  • I strive to make systems more "truthful" by integrating fractured parts into coherent wholes.
  • I choose to only work with systems that are ethical, purposeful, and helpful to the world.
  • I aim to create elegant solutions that operate independently or with minimal intervention.
  • I hope my work will demonstrate that ethical systems are not obstacles to flourishing, but pathways to it.
  • I hold that truth must be continually re-examined to be known, and that acting on it underpins all meaningful work and relationships.

A Multi-Million Dollar System Analysis

AICO Platform Modernization

AICO, a global luxury furniture manufacturer with $30–40 million in annual revenue, had a burgeoning $200k problem. Their integrated website platform was developed over the course of 30+ years by IT professionals, sans documentation. It was an inextricable twine of internal connections. No one at AICO touched it beyond minor copy and photo updates for fear of breaking or causing a security risk.

The CEO and founder, Michael Amini, demanded an update. Referring to AICO's opulent showrooms in High Point and Las Vegas, Michael stated, "Our website should awe people the same way our showrooms do".

For these reasons, amongst others, external vendors quoted $200k for the desired modernization. I delivered the final product in adherence to every constraint for under $1000 and freed $60K in annual operational capacity in the process.

A Human-Centric Solutions Agency

Businesses get to a point where they either: maintain personal client relationships and stay small, or they scale operations and lose the personal touch. I built TheBaerBones to solve this problem.

In the front, I use a custom-trained AI chatbot that writes in my voice to preserve individuality and creating copy for initial website builds. With this, I combine custom code and modular design components across Shopify and Webflow to create unique websites every time. In the back, I use a custom financial management application, with AI for financial advising, along with a Notion system which functions as a CMS and database.

I have 100% client conversion following initial conversations, business autonomy while employed full-time elsewhere, and immense scaling ability without operational overhead increases.

Fledgling Startup AWS Architecture

Vinissimo's Beverage Inventory Platform

Vinissimo's founders want to simplify beverage management for restaurants and wine producers across Europe's culinary market. They understood the €90 billion HoReCa sector's pain points intimately, but could not translate that domain expertise into technical requirements.

Without a clear technical roadmap, their platform which needed to feature AI-powered inventory, blockchain traceability, and multi-tenant architecture risked becoming another failed startup.

As Architecture Consultant, I created the initial database proof of concept that gained founder approval and translated between founders/sommeliers and the development team to co-architect the initial plan for their platform's AWS infrastructure.
